Scholar Lens: An XGBoost-Based Early Risk Detection System Using Student Learning Data
Authors: Anyesha Biswas, Parvathy V Nair
Published: International Journal for Research Trends and Innovation
The system identifies students at risk of underperformance or dropout to enable timely, data-driven academic interventions. It utilizes an optimized XGBoost algorithm, with Bayesian tuning, to process multidimensional student data, including demographic, behavioural, and educational features.

Atenza: Real-Time Attention & Focus Tracking using Webcam Micro-Expressions Concept
Authors: Anyesha Biswas, Parvathy V Nair
Journal: International Journal Of Engineering Research in Computer Science and Engineering (IJERCSE)
Atenza is a real-time, privacy-focused system designed to monitor user attention and well-being during digital tasks. By using a standard webcam, the tool analyzes facial landmarks to track eye gaze stability, blink rates, head posture, and micro-expressions.
